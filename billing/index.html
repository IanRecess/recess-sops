How to Submit Billing Info

Align the organizer's info on the Recess platform and HubSpot so accounting can pay them.

Start
💾 Resuming your draft from —
Step 1

Get the Vendor Billing Form filled out

Send the organizer the form below. Their answers are the source of truth for everything else in this SOP.

Open the Vendor Billing Form
⚠️ GATE — don't skip this: Without this form, the organizer still gets the free product, but they do not get paid. Always push for this before onboarding or building anything in the platform.
💡 TIP: Send it via email with a short note: "We can't pay you out until this is back. Takes 5 min."
Step 2

Who's inputting this?

Type your name. We log it so accounting knows who handled this vendor's onboarding.

Fills the "Inputted By" column (Col A) on the sheet ✓ saved
Type your name to continue
Step 3

Who is the supplier?

Type the Company Name exactly as it appears on the billing form. This goes into the sheet twice — and onto the Recess platform — all identically.

Fills the "Supplier" (Col B) AND "Company Name from Vendor Form" (Col C) — identically ✓ saved
⚠️ Copy exactly: no abbreviations, no LLC if their form doesn't say LLC, no extra words. This same name is going on the Recess platform too — they all have to match.
Type the company name to continue
Step 4

The top half of the form is required — every time

Regardless of how they pay (Bill.com or not), every vendor must fill out the entire Vendor Information section.

⚠️ All 6 fields are non-negotiable:
  • Company Name
  • Contact Name
  • Contact Title
  • Contact Email address
  • Phone Number
  • Company Address
💡 If any of these are blank: bounce the form back to the organizer before you go any further. Don't try to guess or look it up — they fill it out, you don't.
Step 5

Now check: do they have a Bill.com account?

This determines which fields in the bottom half (Method of Payment) they need to fill.

✓ Yes — they have Bill.com
Confirm these two fields are filled:
  • Payment Network ID
  • Bill.com email address
✗ No — no Bill.com
They fill in the full Bank Information for ACH Payment section:
  • Bank Name
  • Bank Account Name
  • Bank Routing Number
  • Bank Account Number
  • Swift Code
Click Yes or No to continue

Step 7

Is this a Supplier or a Supplier - Venue?

A Supplier is one entity that gets paid for everything. A Supplier - Venue has multiple venues that each get paid separately.

Supplier
One entity. One payout. Continue to the next step — no extra work here.
Supplier - Venue
Multiple venues, each paid separately. Needs extra info before continuing.
⚠️ Because this is Supplier - Venue, you'll do:
  • Mark Column E ("Updated in Account Split Reference") as Yes on the main sheet
  • Add a new row to the Account Split Reference tab — vendor name = same supplier name from Step 3
  • Fill in plain-text notes below ↓
  • Date and "Added By" will auto-fill
Goes into the Account Split Reference notes column ✓ saved
Pick Supplier or Supplier - Venue to continue
Step 8

Upload the form + W9

Drag both files here. We'll auto-create a folder named "Supplier Name — Today's Date" inside the Drive and drop both files inside.

ℹ️ What happens when you click Submit:
  • A new folder is created: {supplier name} — {today's date}
  • Folder lives inside the Vendor Onboarding Drive
  • Both files get uploaded into that new folder
  • The folder link auto-fills Column G ("Link to folder") on the sheet
Step 9Optional

Paste the Bulk Import Form link

If this supplier has a Bulk Import Form, paste the link below. Skips fine if there isn't one — this step is optional.

Fills Column F ("Bulk Import Form Link") on the sheet. ✓ saved
✅ Heads up — Column G is automatic. You don't need to paste a folder link anywhere. Column G ("Link to folder") auto-fills with the Drive folder created in Step 8. No action needed.
Step 10Optional · Split payouts

Multiple Billing Sheet (split-payout suppliers only)

Only applies if the supplier manages all offers centrally, but the individual locations get paid out separately after the campaign. If that's the setup, they need to fill out the Multiple Billing template first.

✓ Yes — split payouts per location
Send them the template below to fill out, then paste the link they share back into the input field.
✗ No — single payout to one entity
Skip this step. Leave Column H blank — it doesn't apply.
Open Multiple Billing Sheet template
Fills Column H ("Multiple Billing Sheet Link") on the sheet. ✓ saved
